OTT vs. Movie Theatres

December , 2020

OTT platforms are one of the largest growing sectors of the world . The first ott streaming service was Netflix . It was laumched in the year 1997 . Well initially began as a dvd rental platform over the years it has modified itself into providing original and best content for entertainment whether it is movies or series . Currently , netflix is the biggest ott platform of the world .

Movies theatres also have a very long history starting from late 18th century . It was invincible for nearly over 100 years but now it is getting a tough competetion from ott platforms in all respect . Ott platforms have one advantage over movie theatres , it provides entertainment at your door step .

During the year 2020 , when movie theatres were closed all over world due to covid-19 pandemic , it was a very great opportunity for the ott platforms to grow their business . Movies which were made for theatres , were released on the ott platforms and the number of subscribers of these ott platforms increased significantly . It was a alarming thing for the movie theatre , people have access to severel worldclass entertainment in the form of movies and tv series at their doorstep . Major movie studio Warner Brothers have said that they will release their entire movies of 2021 simultaneously in theatres and in their streaming service HBO Max .
